Paneer Curry Recipe(Dhaba style)


Ingridients

Paneer, oil, coriander, cashews, ginger, Turmeric, Red chilli powder, Salt

How to make paneer curry

First fry the paneer cubes in the pan for sometime, until it turns light red color and then take it off, Wash and soak Cashew Nuts and Melon Seeds.Boil soaked Cashew Nuts and Melon Seeds.In a blender, add boiled Cashew Nuts and Melon Seeds. Make a smooth paste. Keep it aside. Add Oil and Jeera Seeds. Allow to crackle, add chopped Ginger, Garlic and Green Chilli. Saute add chopped Ginger, garicGarlic and Green Chilli. Saute, Add Chilli Powder, Turmeric Powder, Coriander Powder, and Jeera Powder.Add chopped Red Tomatoes and cook till mushy. Add Salt, Paneer Cubes, Tomato Paste and Cashew Magaz Paste. Cook for sometime. Serve in a bowl. Garnish with chopped Coriander.
